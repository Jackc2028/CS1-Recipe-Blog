Ricetta Italiano

Margherita Pizza

Description

A parmesean breaded crust with creamy tomato sauce topped with fresh mozzerella and basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pamesean Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Spread the dough into circular shape
  2. Fold in edges to form crust
  3. Lay out tomato sauce (not on the crust)
  4. Cut up mozzerella into thick slices (not too thick)
  5. Lay out basil

Rigatoni Alla Vodka

Description

Creamy Rigatoni pasta tossed in alla vodka sauce with a touch of garlic and parmesan

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni Pasta
  • Pamesean Cheese
  • Alla Vodka Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Shape pasta
  2. Cook for 30 min
  3. Cook Alla Vodka sauce while cooking pasta
  4. Strain pasta out of pot
  5. Dump pasta into pan with sauce
  6. Cook for 5 more minutes

Chicken Parmesan

Description

Crispy breaded chicken topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzerella

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Flour up chicken breast
  2. Cook chicken breast for 20 min
  3. Cook tomato sauce
  4. Cut up mozzerella into thick slices (not too thick)
  5. Spread sauce onto chicken
  6. Lay out slice of mozzerella ontop of sauce
