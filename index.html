Margherita Pizza

Description

A parmesean breaded crust with creamy tomato sauce topped with fresh mozzerella and basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pamesean Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Spread the dough into circular shape
  2. Fold in edges to form crust
  3. Lay out tomato sauce (not on the crust)
  4. Cut up mozzerella into thick slices (not too thick)
  5. Lay out basil
